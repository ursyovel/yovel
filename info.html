About Me

Hello, my name is Mingsang, and I’m a passionate graphics designer from Nepal with intermediate-level experience. In addition to design, I also have skills in web development and 3D modeling. I'm also a guitar enthusiast and enjoy playing Nepali songs in my free time.

How I Got Started in Design?

→ My journey into design began during the COVID-19 pandemic. With schools closed and a lot of free time on my hands, I started exploring online communities, especially on Discord, while playing games like Minecraft. I came across Minecraft servers that were looking for designers. At the time, I didn’t fully understand what a designer did, but I was eager to learn.

My first exposure to creative work came through uploading videos to YouTube, where I learned basic video editing and started designing thumbnails. Wanting to improve my professionalism, I began studying how to make more appealing and effective thumbnails. My interest deepened when I discovered texture packs for Minecraft, which inspired me to create my own.

From there, I began working as a designer for various servers and received positive feedback on my work. This experience helped me develop a strong foundation in graphic design and sparked a lasting passion for creativity and visual storytelling.

Current life situation (as of May 2025)

→ I have recently completed my board examinations and am now using this transitional period to invest in my personal and professional growth. Currently, I am focusing on learning UI/UX design to broaden my skill set and gain a deeper understanding of user-centered design principles. In addition to UI/UX, I have mapped out a learning plan that includes exploring advanced tools and techniques in graphic design, web development, and 3D modeling.

Alongside my studies, I am actively looking for freelance or collaborative opportunities—not primarily for financial gain, but to apply my skills in real-world scenarios, gain hands-on experience, and enrich my portfolio. I strongly believe that practical exposure and continuous learning are key to becoming a well-rounded creative professional, and I am eager to contribute to projects where I can learn, grow, and make a meaningful impact.
